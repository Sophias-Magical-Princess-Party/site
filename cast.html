Meet Our Cast!

Sophia Carraro is the owner and founder and President of Sophia's Magical Princess Party! Sophia took a short leave from Februrary-August 2019 while she participated in the Disney College Program! Usually you'd see her as most characters offered. She attends College of the Mainland where she is majoring in theatre arts. Sophia knows basic American Sign Language an will definitely put her skills to use! She can't wait to be a part of your princess's special day!

Olivia Bell is the Vice President of Sophia's Magical Princess Party. She is seen mostly as The Snow Princess and The Beauty Princess. In her free time she is very big into acting and singing, as well as working her other job at Break Free Escape Room. Olivia can't wait to help make your child's event magical!

Abigail Heisler is a proud member of Sophia's Magical Princess Party, her roles consist of The Beauty Princess and occasionally the Snow Princess. She is a student at the University of Houston and a big sister to 5 kids. Her favorite part about being a part of Sophia's Magical Princess Party is the good company she keeps around.

Rachel Greeney is just so excited to be a part of SMPP and be a princess! Her love for kids has inspired her to pursue a career in teaching. Rachel is an officer for orchestra at Clear Springs High School and has been involved in theatre for 6 years! Rachel can be seen as many chaeacters including Cinderella, The Scottish Princess, and many others!

Jake Wadkins is proud to be apart of the cast as the Sailor Prince and Prince Charming! Jake has been working with children for 5 years and has been bringing amazing characters to life for the past 8. Jake works as a Para-Professional to young General Ed. and Special Ed. students to help them broaden their social skills and become more loving humans. Jake cannot wait to help your child have a fantastic and magical party on their very special day!

Nayelly Ortiz is a princess at Sophia's Magical Princess Party! She is seen playing as The Polynesian Princess. She loves theatre and is a theatre officer at her high school. She started dancing when she was four and is so excited to dance and have a magical time at your kids' birthdays!

Taylor Greny is so happy to be a part of Sophia's Magical Princess Party! She's seen as The Snow Princess and The Little Mermaid, but can be seen as other characters as well. She's a musical theatre nerd, and is very involved in both Theatre and Choir departments at her school and HSMT. She's so excited to make your child's party absolutely enchanted!

Grace Windhan can be seen playing The Scottish Princess, The Beauty Princess, and The Snow Princess sometimes as well! Grace attends Clear Springs High School as a junior and full time theatre student!
