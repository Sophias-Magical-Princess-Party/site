Meet Our Cast!

Sophia Carraro is the owner and founder and President of Sophia's Magical Princess Party! Sophia took a short leave from February-August 2019 while she participated in the Disney College Program! Usually you'd see her as most characters offered. She attends College of the Mainland where she is majoring in theatre arts. Sophia knows basic American Sign Language an will definitely put her skills to use! She can't wait to be a part of your princess's special day!

Abigail Heisler is a proud full-time performer for Sophia's Magical Princess Party. Her roles consist of The Beauty Princess and Sleeping Beauty. She is a student at the University of Houston and a big sister to 5 kids. Her favorite thing about being a part of Sophia's Magical Princess Party is the good company she keeps around.

Olivia Bell is a seasonal performer for Sophia's Magical Princess Party. She is seen mostly as The Snow Princess and The Beauty Princess. In her free time she is very big into acting and singing, as well as working her other job at Break Free Escape Room. Olivia can't wait to help make your child's event magical!

Rachel Greeney is a seasonal performer for Sophia's Magical Princess Party. Rachel's love of kids and music is the reason they are pursuing a Music Education degree at Sam Houston. You can see her playing Snow White, The Little Mermaid, and The Snow Princess.

Jake Wadkins is a seasonal performer, and is proud to be a part of the cast as the Sailor Prince and Prince Charming! Jake has been working with children for many years and has been bringing amazing characters to life for even longer. Jake has worked as a Para-Professional to young General Ed. and Special Ed. students to help them broaden their social skills and become more loving humans. Jake cannot wait to help your child have a fantastic and magical party on their very special day!

Nayelly Ortiz is a full-time performer at Sophia's Magical Princess Party! She is seen playing as The Polynesian Princess and more characters coming soon. She loves theatre and is a theatre officer at her high school. She started dancing when she was four and is so excited to dance and have a magical time at your kids' birthdays!

Taylor Greny is a seasonal performer, and is so happy to be a part of Sophia's Magical Princess Party! She's seen as The Snow Princess and The Little Mermaid, but can be seen as other characters as well. She's a musical theatre nerd, and is very involved in both Theatre and Choir departments at her school and HSMT. She's so excited to make your child's party absolutely enchanted!

Layah Brown is a proud full-time performer for Sophia's Magical Princess Party! They can be seen playing The Bayou Princess and others. Their entire life revolves around choir, as they are the Choir President at Clear Creek High School. Layah can not wait to bring life and magic into your child's celebration.
