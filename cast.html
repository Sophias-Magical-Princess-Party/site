Meet Our Cast!

Sophia Carraro is the owner and founder and President of Sophia's Magical Princess Party! Sophia took a short leave from February-August 2019 while she participated in the Disney College Program! Usually you'd see her as most characters offered. She attends College of the Mainland where she is majoring in theatre arts. Sophia knows basic American Sign Language an will definitely put her skills to use! She can't wait to be a part of your princess's special day!

Abigail Heisler is a proud full-time performer for Sophia's Magical Princess Party. Her roles consist of The Beauty Princess and Sleeping Beauty. She is a student at the University of Houston and is a Teaching Assistant at Bunker Hill Kindercare. She is also a big sister to 5 kids! Abigail anticipates creating magical moments with your children that will make their dreams come true!

Olivia Bell is a seasonal performer for Sophia's Magical Princess Party. She is seen mostly as The Snow Princess and The Beauty Princess. Rachel Greeney is a seasonal performer for Sophia's Magical Princess Party. Rachel's love of kids and music is the reason they are pursuing a Music Education degree at Sam Houston. You can see her playing Snow White, The Little Mermaid, and The Snow Princess.

Jake Wadkins is a seasonal performer, and is proud to be a part of the cast as the Sailor Prince and Prince Charming! Jake has worked as a Para-Professional to young General Ed. and Special Ed. students to help them broaden their social skills and become more loving humans. Jake cannot wait to help your child have a fantastic and magical party on their very special day!
